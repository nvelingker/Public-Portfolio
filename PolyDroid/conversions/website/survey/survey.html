The purpose of this survey is to gauge the degree to which the user experience is impacted when we remove or change elements from apps. Our goal is to alter applications in such a way that preserves resources (CPU, battery, memory, etc.).

Below you will find 64 versions of 17 applications that have been altered. For each version, there are a pair of videos. On the left is a sequence within the app as it was originally designed, and on the right is the same sequence with some element or elements removed or reduced. We ask that you first read the App-Specific Instructions to get an idea of the type of app this is and the action taking place within the sequence. Then, watch the two sequences and compare the differences. Finally, assign a rating to the changes we made based on the degree to which you are satisfied with our alteration.

A low score indicates that you are not satisfied with the change because we hindered the ability for the user to use and enjoy this application.

A high score indicates that the alteration will not greatly impact the user's ability to use and enjoy this application.
