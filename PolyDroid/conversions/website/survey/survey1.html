Directions

The purpose of this survey is to gauge the degree to which the user experience is impacted when we remove or change elements from apps. Our goal is to alter applications in such a way that preserves resources (CPU, battery, memory, etc.).

Below you will find applications that have been altered. For each version, there are a pair of videos. On the left is a sequence within the app as it was originally designed, and on the right is the same sequence with some element or elements removed or reduced. We ask that you first read the App-Specific Instructions to get an idea of the type of app this is and the action taking place within the video. Then, watch the two videos and compare the differences. Finally, assign a satisfaction rating to the changes we made to the reduced screen in comparison to the original screen.

A low score indicates that you are not satisfied with the change because we hindered the ability for the user to use and enjoy this application. A score of 1 is the most extreme representation of this opinion.

A high score indicates that the alteration will not greatly impact the user's ability to use and enjoy this application. A score of 9 is the most extreme representation of this opinion.
